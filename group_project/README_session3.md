# Group Project — Session 3: Present your findings

This session is about turning your analysis into a short presentation for
the rest of the workshop. Aim for about five minutes per group: enough to
share what you found and invite questions, not enough to cover everything
you tried.

## Questions to answer as a group

- What biological question did your group investigate?
- Who are the participants in your study? (one or two takeaways from your
  Table 1)
- What did you compare, and what did you find?
- Which figure most clearly tells the story? Why that one?
- What are the limitations of your analysis? What could go wrong with the
  conclusion you're drawing?
- If you had another week, what would you do next?
- What did your group learn about working with data this week that you'll
  carry forward?
